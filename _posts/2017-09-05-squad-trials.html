--- title: Squad Trials 2017 excerpt: Trials for the squad will be held on 7th/8th October. ---

Trials for the squad will be held on the Saturday of 0th week (7th October) for all the men's teams and the Sunday of 1st week (8th October) for all the women's team. The times are yet to be confirmed.

Please bring your racket if you have one, but there will be some provided if not. Also, make sure that you arrive before the session begins, as we have a limited amount of time available!
