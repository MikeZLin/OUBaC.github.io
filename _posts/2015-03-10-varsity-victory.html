Congratulations to the Oxford team who pulled out an excellent overall victory over Cambridge last weekend. The team scores were as follows:

Mens 1st Team - 9 - 6
Womens 1st Team - 12 - 3
Mens 2nd Team - 5 - 10
Womens 2nd Team - 12 - 3
Mens 3rd Team - 8 - 7

Overall 46 - 29

In particular well done to the two womens teams for getting a clean-sweep against the Cambridge ladies and the Mens 3rd team for holding their nerve to edge out a very close win. Consider the tabs shoed for another year!
